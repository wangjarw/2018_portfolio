Bio

I am a visual designer with a strong interest in doing graphic design and videography using Adobe Suites. Born with a Taiwanese background, I value the idea of multiculturalism. Hence, I have several design experiences with organizations and programs who mainly work with international newcomers. During the design process, I am interested in doing research, content strategy, storytelling through sketching or illustrations, storyboarding and project management.

My current goal is to learn more about UX/UI design, web design (html/css) and game design (java and Unity) that can potentially enriches myself as a designer. This website is actually coded by myself and I am still really passionate on learning more about this design field.

Interests

I am in love with teaching and learning along with other people. Hence, I have several experiences in teaching, including being a teaching assistant and participating in school funded Techbyte team which is a program that engages new designers to learn design software and programming. Moreover, I am also passionate in learning about different cultures. Currently, I am self learning Japanese and Korean.
